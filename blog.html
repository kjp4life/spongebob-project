A case of the 3 am creative madness

Here I was, hanging out at the Krusty Krab. Then I saw Plankton creeping up with the secret formula. I saw the punk thinking he's slick and decided I would show him I am beyond his simple tricks. I grabbed my spatula, took a patty, and flung that thing at him like an Assassin's Creed trebuchet. And lo and behold, here was my satisfaction when he got bodied by a patty and slammed against the door.
