Spongebob Squarepant's Krusty Krab Work Journal

The Krusty Krab has been my place of work for over twenty years now. It is my home away from home, although Mr. Krabs said I cannot live here. I thought "Wouldn't it be such great idea if you guys can here about what goes on at the Krusty Krab, (Bikini Bottom's Number One restaurant), from time to time!" So, this is if for you, and Squiqward. He misses work a lot so I thought I'd give him updates! I wonder why that is? Below are some of my favorite photos from work. Scroll down to read about my days at work!

Entries

April 24, 2021

Krabs wanted me to come to work earlier today. I was up nice and early to make sure my pet snail Gary had enough food to eat. When I got to the Krusty Krab, it was time for a surprise pop quiz on the Krusty Krab. I would tell you how it went, but the answers are top secret. Later, my best friend Patrick came to visit me, but it seems like all he did was eat the entire restaurant. The furniture included. Once a Krabby Patty is in his face, it's hard to get his attention back.

Later, Mr. Krabs gave me the honor of cleaning the grease traps in the kitchen. Could you beleive it, all for me? There, I found a few bits of burnt-up Krabby Patty. I decided to lump them all together and make a new pet to play with, I call him Bitsy. It seems like time flew right on by because I noticed it was the end of the day soon after. Mr. Krabs let me leave 15 seconds early today. I told him I'll make up the time by working three overtime shifts next week, at half the pay!

April 19, 2021

Today was the best day of work today! One of my closest friends Sandy Cheeks came to the Krusty Krab today. She came to show me her new invention, the new KarateBot 5000. It is the ultimate master at Karate. I don't even think Sandy could defeat it. Below is a picture of her from today. Little does she know that I already have plan up my sleeve. Beside that, I got a massive order today. It was like Christmas. Someone named Shelly P. Lankton ordered 1,000 Krabby Patties. I hope he enjoys them, I made sure I gave each a special kiss to leave a mark of my love of Patties.

April 11, 2021

99.99% of the time, going to work is the best place to be in Sea. I love to work for Mr. Krabs, and I am the best frycook that the Bikini Bottom has ever seen. Today was that 0.01%. I still love Mr. Krabs, but he said something to me that left me almost in tears. I go to work, with a great attitude every day, ready to serve the customers with the utmost joy. Mr. Krabs disagrees. He thinks that while it is good that I am close with the customers, I am getting too personal and that I need to tone it down. This made me sad because The Krusty Krab is my second home, and I love everyone dearly. To say that I can't do that is the hardest thing I may ever have to do.

Working with Squidward, though, does cheer me up. He really is fun to play with. Today, I pretended to be a French Chef while Squidward was an angry patron. Well he might of just been angry. Later, he saw me upset after what Krabs said, and he tried to comfort me by telling me Krabs is a cheapstake old meanie. I said "Hey, don't say that about Mr. Krabs. He's the greatest boss we will ever have." Squidward walked away and took his break after I said that. Now that I think of, Mr. Krabs was only trying to help me at work. He would never say anything to me that wouldn't help the Krusty Krab. With that in mind, I will try to work on being too invasive.

A special thank you to Mr. Krabs for giving me this job so that I can write about it. I want to thank Squidward for being the best cashier anyone could work with. I also want to congratulate the bus driver for getting me to work on time every morning, Old Man Jenkins for being a great customer and support, Mrs. Puff, Larry the Lobster, Mermaid Man and Barnacle Boy, The Flying Dutchman, all the passerbys. Lastly, the pickle jars that never get changed out at work. They're always there for me. Hmmm, am I forgetting anyone? Oh, I don't want anyone to think I forgot them. Well now that I got that out of the way, I want to write my first journal entry about work. Here it goes.
