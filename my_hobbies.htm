Bubble Blowing

I love to blow bubbles with Patrick whenever im free. The feeling of blowing air and creating fantastinc shapes is wonderful and exciting. I have even made a friend called Bubble Buddy when all my freinds were busy. Blowing bubbles takes time and practice to become a bubble blowing champion. The bubble-blowing technique has 9 steps:

  1. Lift your leg up behind you
  2. Spin around for about a second
  3. Stop
  4. Double take 3 times.
  5. Pelvic thrust to your left, then back to your right
  6. Stop on your right foot (Don't forget it!)
  7. "Bring it around town" by spinning your torso in a circle around the air
  8. Do this, and that, and this, and that, this and that, this and that,(Jump around for about 6 seconds)
  9. Blow your bubble lightly for detailed extravagant art
